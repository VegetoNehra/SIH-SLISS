Introduction

SLISS (Student Link of Integrated Smart System) is a powerful yet affordable digital platform designed to simplify academic and administrative processes for schools and colleges. It replaces outdated manual workflows—like maintaining separate ledgers for admissions, fees, hostel allocation, and examination records—with a centralized, cloud-based system that makes data accessible, accurate, and easy to manage. SLISS provides a seamless experience for students, staff, and administrators, creating a single source of truth for institutional data.

Why SLISS

Low-Cost & Scalable: Built with cloud services most institutions already use—no heavy infrastructure or licensing fees. Time-Saving: Eliminates repetitive data entry and manual processes. User-Friendly: Easy for staff to adopt because it uses familiar spreadsheet and form tools. Real-Time Insights: Administrators get a clear, instant view of institutional operations. Efficient & Reliable: Automates key workflows, reduces errors, and ensures smooth communication

How It Works

SLISS connects widely used cloud tools, lightweight scripts, and simple automation to create a low-cost ERP solution: Admission Management: Students apply online through customizable forms, and their data automatically flows into the central database. Fee Collection: Digital payments generate instant, automated receipts, reducing paperwork and queues. Hostel & Library Tracking: Real-time updates keep records accurate and accessible to administrators. Dashboards: Key metrics like admissions, occupancy, and finances are displayed live for decision-making. Secure Access: Role-based logins ensure data security, while regular backups protect sensitive information.
